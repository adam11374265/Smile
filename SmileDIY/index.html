I started crocheting my freshman year of high school. Since then I've learned embroidery and started making jewelry. With all these skills, I wanted to do something for my community. So I crocheted and created items to donate (below). My goal was to make something that others would treasure and have an object that was made with love. I want to share resources for others to learn to create and donate to bring smiles to others. This website is focused on making simple and quick items. Have fun and bring smiles!
