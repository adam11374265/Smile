Beanie

A very simple beanie, using the the double crochet and the chain. I prefer to create ridges, which will be shown after the style with the double crochet. For the first example, I used green yarn for visual clarity and only chained 15 and DC 11 stitches.

Foundation Row: Ch37

This is the height of your beanie. It looks a bit big, but I fold it in the end. Depends on the person.

Row 1: Dc 34

1. Start on the third chain from your hook. (Count 1, 2, 3, stitches.)

2. Double Crochet (dc) 34 stitches

3. Chain 2 after 34 dc

4. Flip your work

Row 3: Repeat

These rows are the actual hat that goes around your head. Repeat these rows until it fits around your head,

Row 24: Fasten off, leaving 24" tail

Cut off a long tail which you will use to connect your hat sides together.

Sew the sides together

Use the tapestry needle and the tail to sew the sides together. Sew the sides by inserting the tapestry in the inner loop of the left side and the outer loop on the right side. Repeat until the entire side is sewn together.

Texture: Ridges

If you wish to make a hat with ridges, dc on the back loop of each stitch
