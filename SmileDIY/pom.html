Pom-Pom

DIY Pom-Pom Maker

Cut out pieces of carboard in a C shape. Make sure they are the same size. These whole C-shape represents the largeness of your pom-pom. The height of the piece is the length of the pom-pom strands,

Place Cardboard Pieces by Side

Place the pieces of cardboard side-by-side.

Wrap Yarn

Start wrapping the yarn around the cardboard. DON'T wrap it tightly.

Cut the Yarm

This is a tricky process. Secure the yarn with your whole hand in the inner part of the C. Cut the yarn in the middle of the cardboard pieces, using them as your guide. To cut the other side, I carefully flipped it while holding the middle. When your finished, set it carefully on a table.

Wrap the String around the Middle

Cut a long piece of string. Pull through in between the carboard pieces and tie it around the middle. This holds the pom-pom together and makes it easier to

Fluff the Pom-Pom

Hold the pom-pom by the string and shake it. Spin it around, so it will get a more natural shape.

Shape the Pom-Pom

Place the pom-pom between the cardboard pieces. Use this to better shape the pom-pom and have more even strands. Rotate the pom-pom and repeat with the cardboard pieces.

Shape the Pom-Pom

Fluff it again, and snip out the little pieces that look imperfect. Then, all done.
