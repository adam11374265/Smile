Stitches

Chain (ch)

This stitch forms the foundation of your project and usually comes after making a slip knot. TIP: Keeping a consistent tension with your stitches will be difficult at first. With more practice, it will become easier.

Counting Stiches

I am always counting in crocheting, making sure I don't accidently add a stitch when I'm making my row. However, when I first began, I had a difficult time telling my stiches apart. This is an example of each stitch.
