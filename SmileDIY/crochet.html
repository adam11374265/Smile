Crochet

This page if a basic overview and understanding of how to crochet. If you wish to go more into depth YouTube is an awesome resource and is how I first learned to crochet.

01

Materials

Basic Materials:

  • Hook 5 mm (1/9) and above
  • Yarn(check the package to make sure it meets the hook size you have)
  • Slip stich (they can be bough or you can use pieces of yarn)
  • Tapestry needle

    • 02

    How to hold your hook

    There are many different ways to hold a hook. I tend to hold it like a pencil, bu tit depends on the person. I reccomend going to YouTube and see which way is most comfortable.

    03

    Making a slip knot

    A slip knot is the starting of point most projects. It ensures the project will not be unraveled. There are many ways to make a slip knot, this is my preferred method.

    04

    Stiches

    Every porject uses stiches. A sticht is the makeup of your entire project. The most common stitches are called 'single crochet' and 'double crochet'.

    05

    Start your first project

    These projects usually consist of double crochet. It's an awesome way to start learning and in the end, it's a beautiful creation.
