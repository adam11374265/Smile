Crochet

This page is a basic overview and understanding of how to crochet and simple projects. Also, YouTube is an awesome resource and is where I first learned to crochet.

01

Materials

Basic Materials:

  • Hook 5 mm (I/9) and above
  • Yarn (check the package to make sure it meets the hook size you have)
  • Slip stich (they can be bought or you can use pieces of yarn)
  • Tapestry needle

    • 02

    How to hold your hook

    There are many different ways to hold a hook. I tend to hold it like a pencil, but it depends on the person.

    03

    Making a slip knot

    A slip knot is the starting of point of most projects. It ensures the project will not be unraveled. There are many ways make a slip knot, this is my preferred method.

    04

    Stiches

    Every project uses stitches. A stitch is the makeup of your entire project. The most common stiches are called 'single crochet' and 'double crochet.'

    05

    Start your first project

    These projects usually consist of double crochet. It's an awesome way to start learning and in the end, it's a beautiful creation.
